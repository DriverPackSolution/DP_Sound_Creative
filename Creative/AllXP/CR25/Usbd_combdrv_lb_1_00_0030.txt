This standalone update provides improvements for your Creative USB Sound Blaster® series audio device on Microsoft® Windows® XP Service Pack 1 (SP1) and Service Pack 2 (SP2), and Microsoft Windows 2000 Service Pack 4 (SP4) operating systems.

Requirements:
Windows XP SP1, Windows XP SP2 or Windows 2000 SP4


Sound Blaster Live!® 24-bit External, Sound Blaster MP3+, Sound Blaster Digital Music, or Sound Blaster Digital Music LX audio device connected

Notes:
Close all applications before installing this update.
While installing this update, do not disconnect your USB Sound Blaster.
After installing this update, do not reinstall any drivers from the Installation CD.
